Frequently Asked Questions

Is using Kivo free?

Yes — it's completely free. We may earn a commission from lenders if you apply through us, but you always see the best options regardless.

Does Kivo affect my credit score?

Nope. We don’t run any credit checks — everything is based on your input and optional Open Banking data.

Is Open Banking safe?

Yes — it’s regulated and secure. You’re always in control, and we never see your login details.

Can I talk to a real person?

Yes — we offer live support and partner with trusted mortgage advisers for more complex cases.

What is a Kivo Score?

It’s our way of helping you understand how close you are to mortgage readiness, using your financial profile and goals.
