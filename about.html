About Kivo

Kivo was born out of frustration — getting a mortgage felt like stepping into a world of jargon, paperwork, and confusing advice. We thought: what if we could simplify it all using technology?

We built Kivo to make mortgage advice accessible, transparent, and actually helpful — powered by Open Banking and AI, but always with a human touch.

We're on a mission to help first-time buyers, movers, and remortgagers get a clearer picture of their mortgage readiness — fast, free, and frustration-free.

We’re not a lender. We’re not here to upsell. We’re your guide — giving you the tools and confidence to take the next step with clarity.

This is mortgage advice reimagined. This is Kivo.
