Our story

We built Kivo because mortgages don't have to be this confusing

The UK mortgage market is worth over £1.5 trillion, yet most first-time buyers go into the process completely blind. They don't know what they can borrow, what lenders actually look for, or how to improve their situation. We decided to change that.

Our mission

Make home buying accessible to everyone, not just those who already know how it works

Right now, having a well-connected family member or a good accountant gives you a huge advantage in the mortgage process. That's not fair. Kivo levels the playing field — giving everyone access to the kind of clear, personalised guidance that was previously only available to the privileged few.

Our journey

How we got here

2023
The idea
After watching a close friend get turned down for a mortgage for reasons nobody had explained to them, we started asking: why doesn't this information just exist, clearly, for everyone?
2024
Building the foundation
Months of research, conversations with hundreds of first-time buyers, and deep dives into how lenders actually make decisions. The Kivo Score methodology is born.
2025
Launch
Kivo launches. Early users start reporting that their scores improved, they got better mortgage advice, and — most importantly — they finally understood what was going on.
Now
Growing together
We're expanding Kivo for Advisors, adding new tools, and working towards a world where nobody is ever left confused about their mortgage journey again.
