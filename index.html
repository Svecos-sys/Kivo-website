Frequently Asked Questions

Is using Kivo free?

Yes — it's completely free to use. We may earn commission from lenders if you choose to apply through us.

Does Kivo affect my credit score?

No. We don’t run any credit checks. Everything is based on your provided info and Open Banking, if you opt in.

Do I have to connect my bank?

Not at all — but it helps us give a more accurate recommendation. You stay in full control.
