How it works

From confused
to confident

Kivo walks you through everything — from understanding what you can borrow, to improving your score, to connecting with a real advisor when you're ready. No jargon. No pressure.

1
Takes 2 minutes
Tell us about yourself

Answer a few simple questions about your income, deposit savings, and what kind of home you're hoping to buy. We'll also ask about your credit history and any existing debts — this helps us give you an accurate picture.

💡 No credit check is run at this stage. We only use what you tell us.
2
Optional but recommended
Connect your bank securely

Use Open Banking to give Kivo a real picture of your finances. We can see your income patterns, regular outgoings, and spending habits — the same things lenders look at. This makes your Kivo Score significantly more accurate.

🔒 Read-only access only. We can never move money or make changes. Your bank login credentials are never shared with us.
3
Instant result
Get your Kivo Score

Our AI analyses your situation against real lender criteria — the same criteria mortgage underwriters use. You'll get a personalised score out of 100, a clear breakdown of your strengths and weaknesses, and specific, actionable steps to improve.

📊 Your score is built on four pillars: Income & Affordability, Deposit Strength, Credit Profile, and Spending Behaviour.
4
Your choice, your pace
Take action — in your own time

Once you have your score, you're in control. Use the personalised advice to improve your position yourself, track your progress as your score updates, or connect with a vetted mortgage broker when you feel ready. We never pressure you.

🤝 If you choose to speak to a broker, they'll already have your Kivo data — no repeating yourself, no starting from scratch.

Open Banking

The safest way to share your finances

Open Banking is a UK-regulated technology that lets you securely share your financial data with approved services — like Kivo. It's the same tech used by Monzo, Starling, and HMRC.

You stay in control. You authorise access and can revoke it anytime from your banking app.
Read-only, always. We can see transactions, never initiate payments or make changes.
Your login is never shared. You authenticate directly with your bank — Kivo never sees your password.
FCA regulated. All Open Banking providers, including ours, are authorised and regulated by the FCA.
