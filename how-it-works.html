How Kivo Works

Step 1: Tell Us About You

Answer a few simple questions about your income, deposit, and housing goals. No credit checks, no paperwork.

Step 2: (Optional) Connect Your Bank

Use Open Banking to securely assess your real affordability based on your actual spending — not just what you tell us. This improves your accuracy and Kivo Score.

Step 3: Get Your Kivo Score

We use AI to analyse your mortgage readiness. You’ll see a personalised Kivo Score and advice tailored to you — not the average buyer.

Step 4: Take Action

We’ll help you plan your next steps — whether that’s improving your finances, connecting with a broker, or starting your application.
